Frequently asked Questions

What is AliExpress Affiliate Marketing?

AliExpress Affiliate Marketing allows you to earn commissions by promoting products sold on AliExpress. When someone makes a purchase through your referral link, you earn a percentage of the sale.

How do I get started with AliExpress Affiliate Marketing?

To get started, sign up for the AliExpress Affiliate Program, create an account, and start sharing your referral links to products. You can promote them via blogs, social media, or websites.

How much can I earn from AliExpress Affiliate Marketing?

Commissions vary by product category, but typically range from 3% to 9%. Earnings depend on the number of sales you generate through your referral links.

Do I need a website to join the AliExpress Affiliate Program?

While having a website or blog can help, it is not required. You can also promote products through social media, YouTube, or other platforms.

How do I track my earnings and performance?

Once you're approved as an affiliate, you can access your affiliate dashboard to track your clicks, conversions, and earnings in real-time.
